Frequently Asked Questions

Orders and Accounts

1. How do I create an Urban Urn account?

Go to the Log In | Sign Up link on the top right hand side of the page. Fill in the required information. Your Urban Urn account will be created. Store your username/password for future use. Happy Shopping!!

2. Do I need an Urban Urn account for placing an order?

Yes, you do. It is a very easy process to create on. We assure you a smooth and hassle free shopping experience with your Urban Urn account where you can transact, add products to your wishlist, prepare custom orders and manage your transaction history with us. Your personal information is 100% secure.

3. How do I know that my order has been confirmed?

You can log in to your Urban Urn Account and check the status of your Orders via the "My Account" link on the top menu.

4. How do I check the status of my order?

You can review the status and other information of all your orders, whether pending or fulfilled, that you have placed with Urban Urn. To check the status of your orders, click on the “Log In | Sign Up” link in the top right of any page on our website to login to your account. This will take you to your Urban Urn account, where you can click on the "Orders" link to view the list of all your orders.

5. What do different order status values mean?

Confirmed: We have received authorization from the payment gateway and we are processing your order. Your order has been shipped/ will be shipped soon.

Delivered: Your order has been delivered at your specified delivery location.

Cancelled: The order has been cancelled.

Shopping

1. What is Wishlist?

In case you like a product that you want to purchase later, we recommend you to add it to your wishlist. You can add multiple products to your wishlist and finally select the ones you one to buy for placing the final order.

2. Can I order an "Out of Stock" Product?

Unfortunately, we do not support these feature as of now. But soon we will enable you to add an "Out of Stock" product to wishlist and we will try to make it available.

3. What is a Custom Order?

We are currently working on this functionality. Once in place, you will be able to suggest your creative ideas to our skilled Artisans and see them come to life. Custom orders, however, being highly customized, take a longer time for fulfilment.

Payments

1. What are the modes of payment supported by Urban Urn?

Urban Urn offers multiple methods to make payments for your order: Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking etc.. All credit/debit card details remain confidential and private. Urban Urn and our trusted payment gateways use SSL encryption technology to protect your card information.

2. Do you support Cash on Delivery?

Unfortunately, We do not support Cash on Delivery as of now. We are working with our logistics partners and vendors to make this feature enabled shortly. Stay Tuned.

3. How do I pay using Credit/Debit/Prepaid Cards?

Once you "Checkout" after adding items to your shopping cart, you are provided with the payment options to choose from.

4. Am I supposed to pay courier charges, local taxes and/or octroi tax?

Courier charges are a part of the delivery charges that exist in your invoice. No additional charges need to be paid for the same.

Any applicable octroi will be payable by the customer. Please pay octroi to the courier company if requested by them in your city. Urban Urn is not responsible for any octroi payments, local taxes.

Delivery

1. What are the delivery charges?

We provide free delivery on most items. Some items may have shipping depending on the nature of the product.

2. What is the estimated delivery time?

Estimated delivery time depends on the order size. Small orders are generally dispatched within 3 working days from the receipt of order.

3. Does Urban Urn deliver internationally?

As of now we do not support delivery outside India. We are working with our logistics partner for enabling the same.

4. How can I track the delivery of my order?

All Urban Urn products are delivered through reputed logistics partners who will provide you with a Tracking ID for your order by which you can track your delivery on the respective websites of our courier/logistics partners. We email you the tracking number after we dispatch your order.

Cancellations, Returns and Refunds

1. Can I cancel an order?

Unfortunately, you cannot do it from our website as of now, once your order is confirmed. You can reach out to us at "support@urbanurn.com" with the grievance and our wizards will get in touch with you to magically ease the process.

2. What is the replacement Policy?

Unfortunately, you cannot do it from our website as of now. You can reach out to us at "support@urbanurn.com" with the grievance and our support team will assist you with the process if the replacement case is genuinely considerable. The fact that each and every Urban Urn product is 100% handcrafted assures authenticity of the art form but the colours might vary marginally owing to the hand work and hence the product might not be exactly the same as in the pictures. However, we try and ensure more than a 99% similarity.

3. Do I get full refund for returned products?

Refunds vary under different circumstances. Please reach out to "support@urbanurn.com" with your grievance and our team will get back to you.
